About Us

Learn more about our institute's rich history, our guiding vision & mission, and meet our distinguished faculty members.

Institute History

CVR College of Engineering was established in 2001 by the Cherabuddi Educational Society with the primary objective of providing top-notch technical education to students in Telangana. The college was founded under the guidance of Dr. Raghava Cherabuddi, an eminent academician and IT visionary.

Over the last two decades, the institute has grown exponentially, achieving autonomous status and becoming one of the most preferred destinations for engineering education. With state-of-the-art laboratories, a vast research ecosystem, and strong industry collaborations, CVR continues to lead in academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be a state-of-the-art institution of engineering in Telangana, matching the global standards of education, technological research, and development. We strive to nurture technically sound, socially responsible engineering graduates prepared for global careers.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality instruction, practical training, and state-of-the-art lab infrastructure. We actively foster research and industry collaboration, enabling graduates to acquire critical-thinking skills and ethical practices to solve real-world problems.
